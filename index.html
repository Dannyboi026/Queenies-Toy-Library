About Us

Built on Love, Play & Community

Queenie's Toy Library was founded with a powerful belief: every child deserves toys that support their development — regardless of family income, background, or ability.

We're an early intervention initiative and parent support group offering free toy lending, sensory play, workshops, and a warm community — always free, always welcoming.

  • Free 3-week toy lending for all families
  • Adapted toys for children with special needs
  • Parent workshops and support groups
  • Welcoming to newcomer and immigrant families

What We Offer

Every service is completely free. Always. For every family in our community.

Workshops & Classes

Parenting classes, child development workshops, and play-based learning sessions for parents and children.

Sensory Play Area

Public Education

Parent Support Groups

Facilitated groups for parents to connect, share, and support one another — single parents and parents of children with developmental disabilities.
